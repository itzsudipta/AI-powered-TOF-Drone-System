🚀 No Satellite Reliance

Problem: Most systems fail in disaster zones due to lack of GPS signals.

Solution: Our system uses non-GPS navigation, allowing drones to operate independently in harsh, blocked environments.

  • Works in GPS-denied areas
  • Reliable in underground / collapsed zones
  • Continuous operation without signal loss

🧠 Dual AI Verification

Problem: Single-model detection often leads to false positives.

Solution: We combine YOLOv8 (vision detection) with an ensemble learning model for double-layer verification.

  • Higher accuracy detection
  • Reduced false alarms
  • Smart decision-making system

🌡️ Multi-Modal Sensing

Problem: Standard cameras fail in smoke, darkness, or heat.

Solution: Uses RGB + Thermal imaging to detect victims even in extreme conditions.

  • Detects in low visibility
  • Heat-based human identification
  • Works in fire & smoke environments
