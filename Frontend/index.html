INNOVATION AT CORE

What makes our system
unique?

No Satellite Reliance

Problem: Most systems fail in disaster zones due to lack of GPS signals.

Solution: Our system uses non-GPS navigation, allowing drones to operate independently in harsh, blocked environments.

  • Works in GPS-denied areas
  • Reliable in underground / collapsed zones
  • Continuous operation without signal loss

Dual AI Verification

Problem: Single-model detection often leads to false positives.

Solution: We combine YOLOv8 (vision detection) with an ensemble learning model for double-layer verification.

  • Higher accuracy detection
  • Reduced false alarms
  • Smart decision-making system

Multi-Modal Sensing

Problem: Standard cameras fail in smoke, darkness, or heat.

Solution: Uses RGB + Thermal imaging to detect victims even in extreme conditions.

  • Detects in low visibility
  • Heat-based human identification
  • Works in fire & smoke environments

WHY OUR MODEL PERFORMS BETTER

Stable accuracy, stronger confidence, better field reliability

Our ensemble-based approach delivers more dependable predictions by combining multiple models instead of relying on a single classifier. That means better robustness, fewer unstable outputs, and stronger performance when real rescue conditions become noisy or uncertain.

Overall Model Accuracy

The Ensemble model achieves the highest accuracy of 84.0%, outperforming both XGBoost and Random Forest. By combining multiple models, it ensures more reliable and stable predictions.

  • Best Performance: Ensemble Model
  • Accuracy: 0.840
  • Advantage: Improved robustness through model combination

XGBoost Analysis

XGBoost performs exceptionally well in detecting non-victim cases, but struggles to identify actual victims, leading to missed detections.

  • Strength: High recall for "Not Present"
  • Weakness: Very low victim recall (~0.05)
  • Impact: Risk of missing critical cases

Random Forest Analysis

Random Forest shows similar behavior to XGBoost, with strong performance for non-victim detection but weak identification of victims.

  • Strength: Good majority class detection
  • Weakness: Very low victim recall (~0.04)
  • Note: Slightly lower overall balance

Ensemble Model Analysis

The Ensemble model significantly improves victim detection while maintaining high accuracy for non-victim cases, making it ideal for real-world deployment.

  • Improved Recall: Better victim detection (~0.19)
  • Balanced Performance: Across both classes
  • Best Choice: Reliable for safety-critical applications
